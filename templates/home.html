About Us – Aromas by HarNoor

I am Harleen, co-founder of Aromas by HarNoor, created with my husband Noorpreet. Our journey began with a simple DIY candle kit and a deep love for fragrances. The moment I lit my first handmade candle, its warmth and aroma touched my heart — and turned a hobby into a passion.

Soon, my family joined in, and what started as a small creative experiment grew into something beautiful. During Diwali, we gifted our candles to loved ones, received heartfelt feedback, and realized that our creations brought joy into people’s homes. That was the beginning of our brand.

Today, Aromas by HarNoor is powered by the constant love and support of our families — the Dugal, the Dhody, and the Ratra clan. From my mother-in-law, sisters, nieces, and relatives to my dear friend Zahida — each one played a part in shaping this dream. We also feel blessed by the heavenly blessings of my father Maninder S. and father-in-law Col. Narinder S.

Our Promise

We don’t just pour wax. We handcraft every candle with intention, warmth, and love so that each one brings happiness, peace, abundance, and positivity into every home it touches.
