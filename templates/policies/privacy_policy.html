At Aromas by HarNoor we are committed to protecting your privacy and ensuring the security of your personal information. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your data when you use our website or make a purchase.

Information We Collect

  • Personal Information: Name, email address, phone number, billing and shipping address.
  • Account Information: Login details, order history, wishlist items.
  • Payment Information: Payment method and payment status (we do not store complete card/UPI data; payments are processed securely by our partners).
  • Usage Data: IP address, device/browser details, and pages visited.

How We Use Your Information

  • Process and deliver orders.
  • Send order updates and confirmations.
  • Improve our products and services.
  • Provide customer support.
  • Detect and prevent fraud.
  • Send promotional emails if you opt in.

Sharing Your Information

We may share limited data with delivery partners, payment gateways, customer support tools, and legal authorities (only if required by law). We never sell your personal data.

Cookies

Cookies help us improve your shopping experience, remember preferences, and manage cart/login sessions. You can disable cookies in your browser settings, though some features may be affected.

Data Security

We use SSL encryption, secure servers, and role-based access controls to protect your data.

Your Rights

You may request access to, correction of, or deletion of your personal data, and you can unsubscribe from marketing emails at any time. Contact us at aromasbyharnoor@gmail.com.

Policy Updates

We may update this policy periodically. Changes will be posted on this page.
